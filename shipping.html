Important Delivery Information

Verify Your Order

Always check the package contents before accepting delivery. If items are damaged or incorrect, refuse the delivery and contact us immediately.

Accurate Address

Provide complete and accurate delivery address including landmarks. Incorrect addresses may cause delivery delays or failed attempts.

Contact Information

Keep your phone number active during delivery. Our courier partner will call you before delivery to confirm your availability.

Secure Packaging

All orders are packed with bubble wrap and secure boxes. Fragile items receive extra protection to prevent damage during transit.

Special Circumstances

No One at Home

If no one is available to receive the delivery, our courier will attempt delivery 2-3 times. You can also arrange pickup from the nearest courier office or reschedule delivery.

Festival & Holiday Delays

During major festivals (Dashain, Tihar) and public holidays, deliveries may take 1-2 additional days. We'll notify you of any expected delays.

Weather Conditions

Adverse weather conditions (heavy rain, landslides, snowfall) in certain areas may cause temporary delays. Safety is our priority.

ID Verification

For high-value orders (above Rs. 10,000), our courier may request ID verification to ensure secure delivery to the right person.
